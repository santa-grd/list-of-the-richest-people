Top 10 Richest People in the World

  • Jeff Bezos

    Jeff Bezos, the founder and former CEO of Amazon, revolutionized the e-commerce industry with his innovative approach. As of 2024, his net worth is approximately $148 billion. Bezos stepped down as Amazon's CEO in 2021 but remains one of the wealthiest individuals due to his significant shares in Amazon and investments in other ventures like Blue Origin, which aims to make space travel accessible. Data sourced from Forbes and Bloomberg.

  • Elon Musk

    Elon Musk, the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, is a prominent figure in technology and innovation. His net worth is around $232 billion as of 2024. Musk is known for his ambitious projects, including the colonization of Mars through SpaceX and advancing electric vehicle technology with Tesla. His influence extends into renewable energy and AI. This information is based on data from Bloomberg and Forbes.

  • Bill Gates

  • Warren Buffett

    Warren Buffett, known as the 'Oracle of Omaha,' is the chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway. His net worth stands at approximately $114 billion. Buffett is renowned for his value investing strategies and long-term approach to investment. His wealth comes from successful investments in various industries, including insurance, utilities, and consumer goods. Data from Forbes and Bloomberg provides this information.

  • Mark Zuckerberg

    Mark Zuckerberg, co-founder and CEO of Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook), has a net worth of about $105 billion. Zuckerberg's fortune is derived from his significant stake in Meta Platforms and its successful ventures in social media and virtual reality. His impact on technology and social connectivity is profound. The data is sourced from Bloomberg and Forbes.

  • Larry Ellison

    Larry Ellison, co-founder and former CEO of Oracle Corporation, has a net worth of approximately $103 billion. Ellison is a key figure in the enterprise software industry and has significantly contributed to Oracle's growth. His wealth is also bolstered by investments in real estate and other ventures. Information is based on data from Forbes and Bloomberg.

  • Larry Page

    Larry Page, co-founder of Google, has a net worth of around $98 billion. His contributions to technology through Google have transformed the internet and search engine industry. Page continues to influence the tech world through various innovations and investments. This information is sourced from Forbes and Bloomberg.

  • Sundar Pichai

    Sundar Pichai, the CEO of Alphabet Inc. (Google's parent company), has a net worth of approximately $82 billion. Pichai has played a crucial role in the growth and success of Google and its parent company. His leadership in technology innovation is well-regarded. Data provided by Forbes and Bloomberg.

  • Sergey Brin

Brin's contributions to search engine technology and his role in Google's development have solidified his place among the world's wealthiest. His continued involvement in technology and investments further enhances his fortune. Information sourced from Forbes and Bloomberg.
